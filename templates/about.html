About Insurance Fraud

Overview

The challenge is to recognize fraudulent Insurance Fraud transactions so that the customers of Insurance companies are not charged for items that they did not purchase.

It contains only numerical input variables which are the result of a PCA transformation. Unfortunately, due to confidentiality issues, we cannot provide the original features and more background information about the data.

Enormous Data is processed every day and the model build must be fast enough to respond to the scam in time.

Insurance Fraud

The cost of fraud for financial institutions is significant, multifold, and rising— fraud costs rose 9.8% from 2018-2018 for institutions with $5-10B in assets, and 7.8% for institutions with <$1B in assets.
